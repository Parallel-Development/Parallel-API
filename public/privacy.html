Privacy Policy for Parallel

This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data collected by Parallel and how it is used. By utilizing Parallel, you consent to the collection and use of your data as described herein. If you do not agree to this policy, please discontinue the use of the bot. For any questions or concerns regarding your data privacy or security, please contact us through our support server.

Information We Collect

Parallel collects only the information provided directly by users while interacting with the bot. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Command inputs.
  • User preferences configured via the bot.
  • Any other data explicitly shared during bot usage.

Any stored Discord messages outside of command prompts are encrypted and accessible only via authorized links. Users have the ability to view and modify most of the data collected through dedicated bot commands.

How We Use Your Data

The data collected is strictly utilized to enable and enhance the functionality of Parallel. It is not repurposed for any activities beyond the essential operation and maintenance of the bot.

Data Security

We prioritize the privacy and security of user data. Parallel ensures that:

  • Online message logs remain encrypted and secure.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized developers only.
  • Users retain full control over data stored by the bot and can manage it via appropriate commands.

We appreciate your trust in using Parallel and are committed to safeguarding your data.
